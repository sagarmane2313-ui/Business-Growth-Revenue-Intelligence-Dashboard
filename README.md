# Business Growth & Revenue Intelligence Dashboard

A Power BI project analyzing Revenue, Profit, Sales Quantity, Customer Distribution, Product Performance, and Country-wise Insights across multiple years.
This dashboard provides clear and actionable insights to help businesses understand their sales growth and performance trends.

🚀 Project Overview

This project is a complete Business Intelligence solution built using Power BI, focused on analyzing:

Total Revenue

Gross Profit

Quantity Sold

Customer Count

Monthly Sales Trends

Product-wise Profitability

Country-wise Units Sold

Revenue decomposition by Country → Subcategory → Product

The goal of this dashboard is to help stakeholders quickly identify growth patterns, market opportunities, and high-performing product categories.

📈 Business Insights Derived

Germany contributes the highest revenue among all countries.

Top products include Quad, Magnum, and Carlota.

Strong revenue jump in 2017 compared to previous years.

Most units sold are concentrated in a single major country segment.

Month-wise revenue trend shows peak performance in April.
